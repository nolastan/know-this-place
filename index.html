Know This Place

Every building has a story.

Know This Place is a community encyclopedia of the built environment — one page per building, weaving together public records, permits, imagery, history, and neighborhood knowledge. Like a wiki, anyone can suggest a change, in plain words; every fact is cited, and every edit is reviewed by a person.

Coverage begins with the Castro / Eureka Valley neighborhood of San Francisco. First pages are on their way.

Know This Place is an open project — the entire site, including how it's made, is public. Source.
