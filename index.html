Know This Place

Every building has a story.

Know This Place is a community encyclopedia of the built environment — one page per building, weaving together public records, permits, imagery, history, and neighborhood knowledge. Every fact is cited, and every edit is reviewed by a person.

Anyone can suggest a change, in plain words — no markup or editing experience needed. Each page carries a link that opens a short form; an automated editor drafts the change against public records, and a person reviews it before anything goes live.

Know This Place is an open project — the entire site, including how it's made, is public. Source.
