56–58 Danvers Street

Corbett Heights · San Francisco, CA 94114

  • Built 1900
  • Two units
  • 2 stories
  • Zoned RH-2
  • Historic status: unevaluated

When this building was photographed in 1909, Falcon Street ran past it to a grocery and saloon. The Market Street extension later took the street, the store and the buildings around it; this one is recorded as still standing as built.

Documented history

  1. April 1906

    The back of the building is probably the one visible at the right edge of the photograph of the earthquake refugee camp at Corbett and Mars.

  2. 1909

    Standing in a photograph of the 18th Street, Danvers and Falcon intersection, close to the grocery and saloon kept by Fred G. Horner at 2 Falcon Street.

  3. 1930s–1940s

    The Market Street extension is cut through the intersection, removing Falcon Street and the buildings around this one.
