# 100–102 Corbett Avenue

Post-war infill at the corner of Ord and Corbett, on a lot created in 1953.
Parcel 2652-002.

## Building facts

- Built: 1953
- Use: multi-family; 2 units; 8 rooms; 2 stories; 2 bathrooms
- Building area: 1,915 sq ft; lot 2,294 sq ft
- Construction: wood frame
- Street numbers: 100 and 102 (one parcel)
- Contractor: Elis J. Ekstrom
- Zoning: RH-2
- Subdivision: McKee Subdivision
- Historic status: CEQA Category B — never formally evaluated
- Assessed 2025: land $179,210 + improvements $376,365 = $555,575

## Documented history

- **1953** — the lot is created by subdividing the parcel at Ord and Corbett
  held by the Cassin family
- **1953** — building completed by the contractor Elis J. Ekstrom, who two years
  later built [385 Corbett Avenue](../385/)

## Permit history

- **2011-07** — eighteen windows and two doors replaced, $21,000 (complete)
- **1991-03** — reroofing, $4,142 (complete)

## Not yet documented

The architect, the first owner, and the original building permit. No sale is
recorded on the current assessor roll.
