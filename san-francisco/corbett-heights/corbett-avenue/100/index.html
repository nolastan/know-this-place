Post-war infill at the corner of Ord and Corbett, on a lot that did not exist until 1953, when the parcel the Cassin family had held since the 1870s was divided.

Documented history

  1. 1953

    The lot is created by subdividing the parcel at Ord and Corbett held by the Cassin family, both sisters having died by 1951.

  2. 1953

    Building completed by the contractor Elis J. Ekstrom, who two years later built 385 Corbett Avenue.
