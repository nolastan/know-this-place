# 301–303 Corbett Avenue

A three-storey building of 1905, standing above the 1906 earthquake refugee
camp at the Slope. Parcel 2659-056.

## Building facts

- Built: 1905
- Use: multi-family; 2 units; 8 rooms; 3 stories; 3 bathrooms
- Building area: 2,498 sq ft; lot 1,337 sq ft
- Construction: wood frame
- Street numbers: 301 and 303 (one parcel)
- Zoning: RH-2
- Last recorded sale: 2019-05-10
- Historic status: CEQA Category B — historic survey result, never formally evaluated
- Assessed 2025: land $1,717,897 + improvements $736,241 = $2,454,138

## Documented history

- **April 1906** — standing above the earthquake refugee camp at Corbett and
  Mars; the open ground below became the Slope community garden
