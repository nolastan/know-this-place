# 123–125 Corbett Avenue

A Spanish Mediterranean house of 1937, one of a pair built side by side on the
lower slope of Corbett Avenue. Parcel 2657-026.

## Building facts

- Built: 1937
- Style: Spanish Mediterranean
- Use: single-family dwelling; 9 rooms; 2 stories
- Building area: 2,732 sq ft; lot 1,947 sq ft
- Zoning: RH-2; Supervisorial District 8
- Street numbers: 123 and 125 (one parcel)
- Contractor: Arthur Quinn (A & B Quinn); no architect
- Build cost, 1937: $4,000
- First owner: D. O'Halloran (1937)
- Historic status: CEQA Category B — never formally evaluated

## Documented history

- **Feb 1937** — building permit filed; stated cost $4,000
- **1937** — house completed by contractor Arthur Quinn
- **1940** — owner valued the house at $6,000 (federal census)
- **1954** — sold by owner at $15,950

## Not yet documented

Whether the house has been altered since 1937, and how the two street numbers
divide the building.
