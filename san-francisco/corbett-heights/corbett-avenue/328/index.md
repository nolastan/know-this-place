# 328 Corbett Avenue

A two-unit building on the hill above the Slope, identified in photographs of
April 1906 and 1909. Parcel 2660-003.

## Building facts

- Built: 1908 (assessor) — but the building is identified in an April 1906 photograph
- Use: multi-family; 2 units; 10 rooms; 3 bathrooms
- Building area: 1,691 sq ft; lot 2,478 sq ft
- Construction: wood frame
- Zoning: RH-2
- Historic status: CEQA Category B — never formally evaluated
- Assessed 2025: land $94,228 + improvements $98,856 = $193,084

## Documented history

- **April 1906** — standing at the top of the photograph of the earthquake
  refugee camp at Corbett and Mars
- **1909** — visible on the hill above the 18th Street, Danvers and Falcon
  intersection, opposite [349 Corbett](../349/), then under construction

## Permit history

- **2017-07** — three street-visible windows replaced, $3,500 (complete)
- **2003-08** — reroofing, $13,500 (issued)
- **2003-03** — porch columns and floor replaced, $7,500 (complete)
- **1990-07** — kitchen, laundry and bathroom remodelled (complete)

## Not yet documented

The builder and first owner. The assessor's 1908 construction date is later
than the April 1906 photograph in which the building is identified. No sale is
recorded on the current assessor roll.
