328 Corbett Avenue

Corbett Heights · San Francisco, CA 94114

  • Built 1908
  • Two units
  • Zoned RH-2
  • Historic status: unevaluated

This building fixes two photographs in place. It stands on the skyline above the 1906 refugee camp, and again on the hill above the 18th Street, Danvers and Falcon intersection in 1909 — a corner the Market Street extension later removed.

Documented history

  1. April 1906

    Standing at the top of the photograph of the earthquake refugee camp at Corbett and Mars; the open ground between here and 303 Corbett became the Slope community garden.

  2. 1909

    Visible on the hill above the 18th Street, Danvers and Falcon intersection, opposite 349 Corbett, then under construction.
