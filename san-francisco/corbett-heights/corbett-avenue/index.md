# Corbett Avenue

The spine of Corbett Heights, climbing from Market Street toward Twin Peaks.
The lower numbers sit in the Park Lane Tract, laid out in 1885; the upper
slopes were still being filled in after the Second World War.

## Buildings documented so far

- [123–125](123/) — a 1937 Spanish Mediterranean house, built for $4,000 by
  the contractor Arthur Quinn with no architect
- [127](127/) — its neighbour, permitted four months earlier at $4,500 for
  the musician Jack Schubel
- [385](385/) — a 1955 architect-designed house on what had been a vacant
  lot, built by Elis J. Ekstrom
