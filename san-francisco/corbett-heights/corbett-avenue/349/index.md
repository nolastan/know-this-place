# 349 Corbett Avenue

A pyramid-roofed house caught under construction in a photograph of 1909.
Parcel 2659-054.

## Building facts

- Built: 1900 (assessor) — but photographed under construction in 1909
- Use: single-family dwelling; 5 rooms; 1 story; 1 bathroom
- Building area: 1,120 sq ft; lot 2,657 sq ft
- Construction: wood frame
- Zoning: RH-2
- Last recorded sale: 2019-11-01
- Historic status: CEQA Category B — never formally evaluated
- Assessed 2025: land $1,232,536 + improvements $528,229 = $1,760,765

## Documented history

- **1909** — photographed under construction, its pyramid roof up, opposite
  [328 Corbett](../328/) on the hill

## Permit history

- **2013-07** — lower-level dwelling unit installed, existing second unit legalized, $90,000 (issued)
- **2007-08** — reroofing, $14,650 (issued)
- **2007-07** — rear siding replaced, $12,500 (issued)
- **2000-09** — renewal of the lower-flat remodel, $6,500 (expired)
- **1999-08** — renewal, $5,500 (expired)
- **1998-09** — renewal, $5,500 (expired)
- **1994-09** — renewal, $5,500 (expired)
- **1991-10** — work continued under the February 1991 plans, $500 (expired)
- **1991-08** — conversion into two units, $23,764 (cancelled)
- **1991-02** — insulation, kitchen cabinets and closet, $15,000 (expired)
- **1989-03** — new redwood decks and stairs, $695 (complete)

## Not yet documented

The builder and first owner, and whether the 2013 second unit was completed.
The assessor's 1900 construction date does not match the 1909 photograph of the
house under construction.
