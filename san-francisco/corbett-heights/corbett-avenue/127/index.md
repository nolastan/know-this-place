# 127 Corbett Avenue

A two-unit Spanish Mediterranean building of 1937, the earlier of the pair
built side by side here. Also given as 9 Hattie Street. Parcel 2657-025.

## Building facts

- Built: 1937
- Style: Spanish Mediterranean
- Use: two units ("Flats & Duplex"); 8 rooms; 1 story
- Building area: 1,450 sq ft; lot 1,733 sq ft
- Zoning: RH-2; Supervisorial District 8
- Also known as: 9 Hattie Street (the assessor records only 127 Corbett)
- Contractor: not recorded; no architect
- Estimated cost, 1936: $4,500
- First owner: J. H. Schubel (1937)
- Historic status: CEQA Category B — never formally evaluated

## Documented history

- **Jul 1936** — lot sold to J. H. Schubel
- **Oct 1936** — building permit filed; estimated cost $4,500
- **1937** — building completed
- **1940** — owner valued the building at $10,000 (federal census)

## Not yet documented

Who built the house, whether the Hattie Street number is a second entrance or
a separate unit, and any alterations since 1937.
