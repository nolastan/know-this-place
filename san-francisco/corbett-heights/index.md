# Corbett Heights

The hillside neighborhood around Corbett Avenue, between Market Street and
Twin Peaks, north-west of the Castro. Its streets include Corbett Avenue,
Ord, Hattie, Danvers, Mars, Romain and Museum Way.

## How the land was divided

- McKee Subdivision — 40 lots; central street Clara renamed Ord in 1909
- Park Lane Tract — from 1885
- Street numbers changed across the neighborhood in 1909, so an address in an
  older record often does not match today's (1200 Ashbury became 1200
  Clayton; 110 Clara became 110 Ord)
