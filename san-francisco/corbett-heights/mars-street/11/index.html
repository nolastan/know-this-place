11–23 Mars Street

Corbett Heights · San Francisco, CA 94114

  • Built 1909
  • Single-family house
  • 2 stories
  • Zoned RH-2
  • Relocated building

This house did not start out on Mars Street. It was moved here from Falcon Street, one of the streets erased when Market Street was cut through the neighborhood, and photographs of Mars taken about 1921 show its lot still empty.

Documented history

  1. 1909

    Construction date recorded by the assessor — predating the move to this site.

  2. c. 1921

    The lot is still vacant in two photographs of Mars Street taken before the street improvements.

  3. After 1921

    Building moved to Mars Street from Falcon Street, which was expunged by the Market Street extension. Its former line is now the Slope.
