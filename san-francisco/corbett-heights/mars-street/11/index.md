# 11–23 Mars Street

A house recorded as built in 1909 that was moved to Mars Street from Falcon
Street. One parcel over three street numbers. Parcel 2653-022.

## Building facts

- Built: 1909 (assessor); moved to this site after about 1921
- Use: single-family dwelling; 12 rooms; 2 stories; 2 bathrooms
- Building area: 2,475 sq ft; lot 2,230 sq ft
- Construction: wood frame
- Street numbers: 11, 15 and 23 (one parcel)
- Zoning: RH-2
- Last recorded sale: 2021-12-16
- Historic status: CEQA Category B — never formally evaluated
- Assessed 2025: land $1,894,256 + improvements $811,824 = $2,706,080

## Documented history

- **1909** — construction date recorded by the assessor, predating the move
- **c. 1921** — the lot is still vacant in two photographs of Mars Street
- **after 1921** — building moved here from Falcon Street, which was expunged
  by the Market Street extension; its former line is now the Slope

## Permit history

- **2026-05** — tempered glass guard rail at third-floor balcony, $3,000 (issued)
- **2024-11** — third-floor kitchen remodel and new half bathroom, $50,000 (issued)
- **2023-08** — reroofing, $24,000 (complete)
- **2023-07** — voluntary seismic strengthening of the cripple wall, $50,000 (complete)
- **2023-03** — top-floor bathroom remodel, $28,192 (issued)
- **2022-04** — kitchen and bathroom remodelled in kind, $42,732 (complete)
- **2011-03** — kitchen removed from an illegal first-floor unit, $1,000 (issued)
- **1996-10** — reroofing, $2,000 (expired)

## Not yet documented

The year the building was moved, its address on Falcon Street, and who moved
it. The assessor records two storeys while recent permits describe a third
floor.
