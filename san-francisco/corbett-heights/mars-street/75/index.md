# 75 Mars Street

A hillside house rebuilt and enlarged between 2010 and 2017, beside the public
stair known as Shiloh's Way. Parcel 2653-016.

## Building facts

- Built: 1940 (assessor, pre-dating the rebuild)
- Use: single-family dwelling; 4 stories; 4 bathrooms
- Building area: 3,213 sq ft; lot 1,354 sq ft
- Construction: wood frame
- Zoning: RH-2
- Last recorded sale: 2013-10-11
- Historic status: CEQA Category C — not a historical resource
- Assessed 2025: land $918,166 + improvements $2,179,710 = $3,097,876

## Shiloh's Way

The stair beside the house is a public route between Corbett Avenue and Mars
Street, built as part of the same project on what had been an overgrown
hillside, and lit, planted and maintained by the property's owners rather than
the city. It is named after their dog.

## Permit history

- **2017-08** — final inspection closing out the rebuild (complete)
- **2015-12** — basement and first-floor plan modified, $55,000 (issued)
- **2015-03** — fourth-floor elevator eliminated, $24,000 (issued)
- **2014-05** — shoring and excavation, $118,300 (complete)
- **2013-10** — interior changes to reduce excavation (issued)
- **2010-06** — renovation with a new floor and basement garage, $745,000 (complete)
- **2004-03** — thirteen windows replaced, $6,000 (complete)
- **2004-01** — kitchen cabinets and counters replaced, $10,000 (complete)
- **1998-08** — dry rot repair, $445 (expired)

## Not yet documented

The original 1940 building and who built it, and the year Shiloh's Way opened.
