# 4 Mars Street

A one-storey house of 1907 at the 17th Street end of Mars Street, on the
stretch rebuilt under the 1922 street improvement order. Parcel 2654-002.

## Building facts

- Built: 1907
- Use: single-family dwelling; 4 rooms; 1 story; 1 bathroom
- Building area: 1,167 sq ft; lot 2,435 sq ft
- Construction: wood frame
- Zoning: RH-2
- Last recorded sale: 1989-11-02
- Historic status: CEQA Category B — never formally evaluated
- Assessed 2025: land $505,934 + improvements $335,624 = $841,558

## Documented history

- **c. 1921** — standing at the corner of Mars and 17th, intact as built, in a
  photograph taken before the street improvements

## Permit history

- **2015-10** — horizontal addition in the rear yard, $140,000 (issued)
- **2011-03** — bathroom renovation, $15,000 (complete)
- **2008-12** — six front windows replaced, $2,000 (complete)
- **2004-04** — reroofing, $6,000 (complete)
- **2000-12** — two-storey addition, $90,000 (cancelled)
- **1989-12** — re-plastering and window repair, $4,500 (complete)
- **1986-12** — footing poured under the existing foundation, $4,100 (complete)

## Not yet documented

The builder and first owner, and whether the 2015 rear addition was carried
out.
