Documented history

    Standing in a photograph of Mars Street taken from Kite Hill.

    Visible above the earthquake refugee camp at Corbett and Mars.

  3. 1912

    Photographed at close range with its neighbours at 56 and 64 Mars.

  4. After 1921

    Greatly altered; a photograph taken from 17th Street that year still shows it unaltered.
