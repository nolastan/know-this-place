# 33–37 Mars Street

A three-unit building on the east side of Mars Street, standing since about
1900 and much altered. One parcel over three street numbers. Parcel 2653-021.

## Building facts

- Built: 1900 (assessor)
- Use: multi-family; 3 units; 8 rooms; 2 stories; 3 bathrooms
- Building area: 2,000 sq ft; lot 2,469 sq ft
- Construction: wood frame
- Street numbers: 33, 35 and 37 (one parcel)
- Zoning: RH-2
- Last recorded sale: 2000-04-18
- Historic status: CEQA Category B — never formally evaluated
- Assessed 2025: land $768,484 + improvements $499,508 = $1,267,992

## Documented history

- **c. 1921** — standing in a photograph of Mars Street taken before the street
  improvements; greatly altered since

## Permit history

- **2022-01** — reroofing, $5,859 (issued)
- **2011-07** — handrail on outside stairs, $475 (complete)
- **2006-07** — windows replaced in kind, $4,000 (complete)
- **2003-04** — bathroom remodel, $4,000 (complete)
- **2002-03** — kitchen remodel, $5,000 (complete)
- **2000-06** — kitchen cabinets, windows, bathroom, spiral stair, $8,000 (complete)
- **1987-02** — horizontal addition, $5,000 (complete)
- **1984-03** — accessory building, $9,000 (expired)
- **1983-06** — exterior deck, $3,000 (issued)
- **1981-07** — work recorded without a description, $50,000 (complete)

## Not yet documented

Which alterations produced the present appearance, and what the $50,000 permit
of 1981 covered. The assessor's 1900 date is a round year and has not been
checked against a permit.
