# 32 Mars Street

A small single-storey house of 1925. Parcel 2654-004.

## Building facts

- Built: 1925
- Use: single-family dwelling; 4 rooms; 1 story; 1 bathroom
- Building area: 835 sq ft; lot 2,735 sq ft
- Construction: wood frame
- Zoning: RH-2
- Historic status: CEQA Category B — never formally evaluated
- Assessed 2025: land $139,375 + improvements $159,290 = $298,665

## Documented history

- **1921** — the lot is still open in a photograph taken from 17th Street
  looking south along Mars

## Permit history

- **2006-01** — ten replacement windows, $7,520 (complete)
- **1994-09** — reroofing, $4,300 (expired)

## Not yet documented

The builder, architect and first owner, and the original building permit. No
sale is recorded on the current assessor roll.
