# 41–43 Mars Street

A two-unit building recorded as built in 1890 — the earliest construction date
the assessor gives on Mars Street. Parcel 2653-033.

## Building facts

- Built: 1890 (assessor)
- Use: multi-family; 2 units; 6 rooms; 1 story; 2 bathrooms
- Building area: 1,320 sq ft; lot 1,912 sq ft
- Construction: wood frame
- Street numbers: 41 and 43 (one parcel)
- Zoning: RH-2
- Last recorded sale: 2018-04-26
- Historic status: CEQA Category B — never formally evaluated
- Assessed 2025: land $1,085,000 + improvements $465,000 = $1,550,000

## Documented history

- **c. 1921** — standing in a photograph of Mars Street; some alteration since

## Permit history

- **2024-01** — bathroom remodel, $33,000 (complete)
- **2022-11** — five street-visible windows, cancelled
- **2016-01** — rear deck ledger and siding repaired, $5,000 (complete)
- **2000-02** — reroofing, $7,300 (expired)
- **1986-05** — bedroom added at first and second floors with rear deck, $20,000 (complete)

## Not yet documented

The builder and first owner, and the original building permit behind the
assessor's 1890 date.
