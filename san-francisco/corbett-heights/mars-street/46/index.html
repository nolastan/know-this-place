San Francisco Corbett Heights Mars Street 46–48

46–48 Mars Street

Corbett Heights · San Francisco, CA 94114

  • Built 1903
  • House with rear cottage
  • 2 stories
  • Zoned RH-2
  • Historic status: unevaluated

Among the earliest buildings documented on Mars Street: it stands in a photograph taken from Kite Hill about 1895, and again in the photograph of the 1906 earthquake refugee camp on the slope below.

Documented history

  1. c. 1895

    Standing on the west side of Mars in a photograph taken from Kite Hill, when the corner at 17th Street was still open ground.

  2. April 1906

    Visible above the earthquake refugee camp at Corbett and Mars, in a photograph taken from the direction of Caselli and 18th.

  3. 2005

    The rear cottage renumbered from 48A to 46, giving the parcel its present pair of numbers.
