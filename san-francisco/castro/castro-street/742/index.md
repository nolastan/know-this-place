# 742 Castro Street

A four-unit building of **1895** — the earliest construction date among the
700-block buildings documented so far — with the block's deepest permit
record, running from 1978 to 2025. Parcel 2752-015.

## The building

- Built 1895 (assessor roll)
- Four residential units ("Apartment 4 units or less" property class),
  14 rooms, 4 baths, 2 stories
- Building area 2,700 sq ft; lot 3,125 sq ft
- Wood-frame construction (assessor construction type D)
- Zoning RH-2; Supervisorial District 8
- Historic status: CEQA Category B — never formally evaluated. Not a
  designated city landmark; not within an Article 10 or 11 historic district
  (SF Planning, Historic Resource Status by Parcel)

## Permit history

oldest, filed in November 1978, was cancelled and carries no description; the
substantive record begins in 1983 and continues to 2025.

## Ownership and value

The assessor's roll records the most recent sale on **April 7, 2008**. The
2025 assessed value is $673,448 (land) plus $730,874 (improvements) —
$1,404,322 total.
