# 748 Castro Street

A three-unit building of **1900**, in the same ownership since 1987 — the
longest holding period among the 700-block buildings documented so far — and
with only three permits on file, the shortest permit record on the block.
Parcel 2752-018.

## The building

- Built 1900 (assessor roll)
- Three residential units ("Apartment 4 units or less" property class),
  14 rooms, 3 baths, 2 stories
- Building area 2,510 sq ft; lot 3,123 sq ft
- Wood-frame construction (assessor construction type D)
- Zoning RH-2; Supervisorial District 8
- Historic status: CEQA Category B — never formally evaluated. Not a
  designated city landmark; not within an Article 10 or 11 historic district
  (SF Planning, Historic Resource Status by Parcel)

## Permit history

Three permits are on file with the Department of Building Inspection, all
small. Two of the three relate to a single notice of violation issued in the
early 2000s.

- **2003** — work to comply with notice of violation 200235554 ($1,000;
  issued) —
  [permit 200303058816](https://dbiweb02.sfgov.org/dbipts/default.aspx?page=Permit&PermitNumber=200303058816)
- **2004** — renewal of the 2003 application to comply with the same notice
  ($1,000) —
  [permit 200405073340](https://dbiweb02.sfgov.org/dbipts/default.aspx?page=Permit&PermitNumber=200405073340)
- **2014** — foundation repair ($3,000) —
  [permit 201407090789](https://dbiweb02.sfgov.org/dbipts/default.aspx?page=Permit&PermitNumber=201407090789)
