A modern building in a historic district

The parcel lies inside the Castro & Liberty Streets Historic District, whose period of significance is 1897–1906 — so this building, completed in 1980, postdates the district by roughly three quarters of a century. It nonetheless carries CEQA Category A status, which Planning assigns at parcel level to everything inside the district boundary. Whether the building counts as contributing or non-contributing is not established by the data used here.
