Permit history

Thirteen permits are on file across the three street numbers — the Department of Building Inspection files the same permit numbers under each. Dry rot dominates the record: three permits between 2009 and 2010 totalling $62,500 addressed rot at the rear wall and stairs, and a 1983 permit had already cited a dry-rotted roof.
