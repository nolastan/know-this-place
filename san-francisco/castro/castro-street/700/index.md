# 700 Castro Street

A 12-unit apartment building at the corner of 700 Castro Street, built in
**1927** — later than the Victorian and Edwardian houses that make up most of
the block, and the largest building on it at 7,665 square feet. Parcel
2752-035.

## The building

- Built 1927 (assessor roll)
- 12 residential units ("Apartment 5 to 14 Units" property class), 30 rooms,
  12 baths, 3 stories
- Building area 7,665 sq ft; lot 3,080 sq ft
- Wood-frame construction (assessor construction type D)
- Zoning RH-3; Supervisorial District 8
- Historic status: CEQA Category B — never formally evaluated. Not a
  designated city landmark; not within an Article 10 or 11 historic district
  (SF Planning, Historic Resource Status by Parcel)

## Permit history

Eleven permits are on file with the Department of Building Inspection. The
2016 soft-story retrofit is the notable one: San Francisco's mandatory
seismic program requires wood-frame buildings of three or more stories with
five or more units to strengthen weak ground floors, and this building's
retrofit was carried out under that program.

- **1993** — reroofing ($8,300; expired) —
  [permit 9310423](https://dbiweb02.sfgov.org/dbipts/default.aspx?page=Permit&PermitNumber=9310423)
- **2002** — kitchen remodel in two units ($8,000; issued) —
  [permit 200205075983](https://dbiweb02.sfgov.org/dbipts/default.aspx?page=Permit&PermitNumber=200205075983)
- **2003** — deck, garbage chute deck and egress stairs repaired and upgraded
  ($7,500) —
  [permit 200311059406](https://dbiweb02.sfgov.org/dbipts/default.aspx?page=Permit&PermitNumber=200311059406)
- **2010** — kitchen cabinets, counters and appliance locations changed in one
  unit ($10,000) —
  [permit 201007297730](https://dbiweb02.sfgov.org/dbipts/default.aspx?page=Permit&PermitNumber=201007297730)
- **2016** — soft story retrofit per IEBC appendix A4 ($50,000) —
  [permit 201609076903](https://dbiweb02.sfgov.org/dbipts/default.aspx?page=Permit&PermitNumber=201609076903)
- **2016** — dwelling unit addition per ordinance 162-16 ($120,000; filed, not

  [permit 201612296164](https://dbiweb02.sfgov.org/dbipts/default.aspx?page=Permit&PermitNumber=201612296164)

  [permit 201708306436](https://dbiweb02.sfgov.org/dbipts/default.aspx?page=Permit&PermitNumber=201708306436)

  [permit 202504255245](https://dbiweb02.sfgov.org/dbipts/default.aspx?page=Permit&PermitNumber=202504255245)

## Ownership and value

The 2025 assessed value is $72,012 (land) plus $321,360 (improvements) —
$393,372 total. Unlike the single-family houses on this block, the assessed
improvement value here exceeds the land value. No sale date is recorded on
the 2025 roll.
