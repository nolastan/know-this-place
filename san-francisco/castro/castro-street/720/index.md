# 720 Castro Street

A two-storey house of **1901** on the even side of the 700 block, sold in
December 2024 — the most recent transfer among the buildings documented here.
Parcel 2752-008.

## The building

- Built 1901 (assessor roll)
- Single-family dwelling, 6 rooms, 2 baths, 2 stories
- Building area 2,020 sq ft; lot 3,123 sq ft
- Wood-frame construction (assessor construction type D)
- Zoning RH-2; Supervisorial District 8
- Historic status: CEQA Category B — never formally evaluated. Not a
  designated city landmark, and unlike the odd side of the block the parcel
  is not inside a historic district (SF Planning, Historic Resource Status by
  Parcel; Historic Districts)

  [permit 201202083778](https://dbiweb02.sfgov.org/dbipts/default.aspx?page=Permit&PermitNumber=201202083778)

## Ownership and value

The assessor's roll records the most recent sale on **December 10, 2024**.
The 2025 assessed value is $1,995,000 (land) plus $855,000 (improvements) —
$2,850,000 total. Because California reassesses property at its sale price,
this figure reflects the 2024 transfer rather than a long-held Proposition 13
base, and so sits far above neighbours that have not changed hands recently.
