# 732 Castro Street

A two-storey house of **1901** with eleven rooms, carrying the lowest assessed
value among the buildings documented on the 700 block. Its largest recorded
alteration is a horizontal addition permitted in 1990. Parcel 2752-011.

## The building

- Built 1901 (assessor roll)
- Single-family dwelling, 11 rooms, 3 baths, 2 stories
- Building area 2,319 sq ft; lot 3,125 sq ft
- Wood-frame construction (assessor construction type D)
- Zoning RH-2; Supervisorial District 8
- Historic status: CEQA Category B — never formally evaluated. Not a
  designated city landmark, and the parcel is not inside a historic district
  (SF Planning, Historic Resource Status by Parcel; Historic Districts)

Three permits are on file with the Department of Building Inspection.

- **1990** — horizontal addition ($65,000) —
  [permit 9004677](https://dbiweb02.sfgov.org/dbipts/default.aspx?page=Permit&PermitNumber=9004677)
- **2001** — eight windows replaced like for like, wood for wood, no
  structural changes ($11,000; expired) —
  [permit 200112064748](https://dbiweb02.sfgov.org/dbipts/default.aspx?page=Permit&PermitNumber=200112064748)
- **2014** — kitchen remodelled with new sheetrock, insulation and cabinets;
  plumbing and electrical brought up to code ($15,000) —
  [permit 201401095929](https://dbiweb02.sfgov.org/dbipts/default.aspx?page=Permit&PermitNumber=201401095929)
