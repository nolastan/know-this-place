# 740 Castro Street

A three-story house of **1900** on the 700 block of Castro Street, identified
as a historic resource in the city's planning survey and the most heavily
altered building on the block: three permits between 2019 and 2021 total
$620,000. Parcel 2752-014.

## The building

- Built 1900 (assessor roll)
- Single-family dwelling, 11 rooms, 2 baths, 3 stories
- Building area 3,050 sq ft; lot 3,123 sq ft
- Wood-frame construction (assessor construction type D)
- Zoning RH-2; Supervisorial District 8
- Historic status: **CEQA Category A — a historical resource is present**,
  determined by historic survey. Not a designated city landmark; not within an
  Article 10 or 11 historic district (SF Planning, Historic Resource Status by
  Parcel)

## Permit history

Twelve permits are on file with the Department of Building Inspection. The
property last sold on July 31, 2019; the three permits filed between October
2019 and May 2021 carry estimated costs totalling $620,000.

- **1990** — cap footings, two additional shear walls ($8,000; expired) —
  [permit 9010972](https://dbiweb02.sfgov.org/dbipts/default.aspx?page=Permit&PermitNumber=9010972)
- **1993** — rotted wall removed and replaced, footing and stair rail raised
  ($7,000) —
  [permit 9313731](https://dbiweb02.sfgov.org/dbipts/default.aspx?page=Permit&PermitNumber=9313731)
- **1995** — reroofing ($9,000) —
  [permit 9505249](https://dbiweb02.sfgov.org/dbipts/default.aspx?page=Permit&PermitNumber=9505249)
- **1997** — bathroom remodel with new plumbing and electrical ($5,000) —
  [permit 9712921](https://dbiweb02.sfgov.org/dbipts/default.aspx?page=Permit&PermitNumber=9712921)
- **1998** — horizontal addition ($30,000) —
  [permit 9808850](https://dbiweb02.sfgov.org/dbipts/default.aspx?page=Permit&PermitNumber=9808850)
- **2004** — roof replaced with a roof deck serving a second-floor bedroom
  ($90,000; cancelled) —
  [permit 200410146871](https://dbiweb02.sfgov.org/dbipts/default.aspx?page=Permit&PermitNumber=200410146871)
- **2011** — kitchen, powder room and pantry remodel ($60,000) —
  [permit 201106309326](https://dbiweb02.sfgov.org/dbipts/default.aspx?page=Permit&PermitNumber=201106309326)
- **2019** — interior remodel of two baths, half bath relocated, new sliding
  doors at den ($100,000) —
  [permit 201910114224](https://dbiweb02.sfgov.org/dbipts/default.aspx?page=Permit&PermitNumber=201910114224)
- **2021** — revised den access to rear patio, new rear yard landscaping
  ($160,000) —
  [permit 202101072244](https://dbiweb02.sfgov.org/dbipts/default.aspx?page=Permit&PermitNumber=202101072244)
- **2021** — garage expansion and foundation replacement, living space added
  at basement ($360,000) —
  [permit 202105210825](https://dbiweb02.sfgov.org/dbipts/default.aspx?page=Permit&PermitNumber=202105210825)

A $0 withdrawn revision (2022) and a $100 skylight revision (2000) are
omitted above.

## Ownership and value

The assessor's roll records the most recent sale on **July 31, 2019**. The
2025 assessed value is $3,200,006 (land) plus $1,658,579 (improvements) —
$4,858,585 total, the highest on the block among the buildings documented so
far.
