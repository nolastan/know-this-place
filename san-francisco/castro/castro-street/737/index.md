# 737 Castro Street

A three-storey house of **1900** inside the Castro & Liberty Streets Historic
District, remodelled between 2005 and 2007 in a campaign of five permits
totalling $270,000 — including three new dormers and the conversion of the
attic to a bedroom. Parcel 3603-068.

## The building

- Built 1900 (assessor roll)
- Single-family dwelling, 7 rooms, 2 baths, 3 stories
- Building area 2,732 sq ft; lot 2,622 sq ft
- Wood-frame construction (assessor construction type D)
- Zoning RH-2; Supervisorial District 8
- Historic status: **CEQA Category A — a historical resource is present**,
  on the grounds that the parcel falls within a California Register historic
  district

## Historic district

The parcel lies inside the **Castro & Liberty Streets Historic District**,
period of significance 1897–1906. The district is **eligible** for the
California Register; it is not listed on the National Register, and it is not
an Article 10 local landmark district.

## Permit history

Twelve permits are on file with the Department of Building Inspection. Five
of them, filed between July 2005 and October 2007, carry estimated costs
totalling $270,000 and together account for most of the visible alteration —
dormers, an attic conversion, kitchen and bathroom work, half a new
foundation and a replaced retaining wall.

- **1993** — reroofing ($5,400; expired) —
  [permit 9312773](https://dbiweb02.sfgov.org/dbipts/default.aspx?page=Permit&PermitNumber=9312773)
- **2002** — excavation, foundation, framing, electrical, plumbing and
  finishes to kitchen and bath ($35,000; cancelled) —
  [permit 9708099](https://dbiweb02.sfgov.org/dbipts/default.aspx?page=Permit&PermitNumber=9708099)
- **2005** — two bathrooms remodelled; kitchen lighting, finishes and
  insulation ($40,000) —
  [permit 200507056843](https://dbiweb02.sfgov.org/dbipts/default.aspx?page=Permit&PermitNumber=200507056843)
- **2005** — about half the existing foundation replaced, concrete cap on the
  north side ($15,000) —
  [permit 200508220845](https://dbiweb02.sfgov.org/dbipts/default.aspx?page=Permit&PermitNumber=200508220845)
- **2005** — kitchen and baths remodelled; three new dormers in the attic
  space; attic converted to a primary bedroom ($175,000) —
  [permit 200509082285](https://dbiweb02.sfgov.org/dbipts/default.aspx?page=Permit&PermitNumber=200509082285)
- **2006** — revision showing new framing at the rear room and lower back
  room ($20,000) —
  [permit 200604189218](https://dbiweb02.sfgov.org/dbipts/default.aspx?page=Permit&PermitNumber=200604189218)
- **2007** — temporary bracing of the rear wall between 737 and 741 Castro
  Street ($2,000) —
  [permit 200702023180](https://dbiweb02.sfgov.org/dbipts/default.aspx?page=Permit&PermitNumber=200702023180)
- **2007** — revision showing replacement of the retaining wall ($20,000) —
  [permit 200710034463](https://dbiweb02.sfgov.org/dbipts/default.aspx?page=Permit&PermitNumber=200710034463)
- **2020** — new L-shaped rear deck, 167 sq ft (cancelled; no cost recorded) —
  [permit 202011219480](https://dbiweb02.sfgov.org/dbipts/default.aspx?page=Permit&PermitNumber=202011219480)

A $1 renewal for final inspection and two $0 sidewalk and street-space
permits are omitted above.

## Ownership and value

The assessor's roll records the most recent sale on **April 1, 2022**. The
2025 assessed value is $2,807,955 (land) plus $1,203,409 (improvements) —
$4,011,364 total.
