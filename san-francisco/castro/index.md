# Castro / Eureka Valley

One neighborhood in central San Francisco, known by more than one name. It
sits roughly between Market Street to the northeast, the Douglass Street
hillside to the west, 22nd Street to the south, and Church Street to the
east, and its housing stock is heavily Victorian and Edwardian.

## Castro or Eureka Valley?

Both — they are the same place. **Eureka Valley** is the older name, for the
valley the neighborhood occupies; **the Castro** is what almost everyone
calls it now, after its main street. This site files addresses under "Castro"
and notes Eureka Valley alongside it.

The city's own records are no more consistent, which is worth knowing when
reading the sources cited on each building's page:

- Addressing system and planning datasets: "Castro/Upper Market"
- Assessor's roll: "Eureka Valley/Dolores Heights"
- In everyday use: "the Castro"
