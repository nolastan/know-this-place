# Castro / Eureka Valley

The Castro — formally Eureka Valley — sits in central San Francisco,
roughly bounded by Market Street to the northeast, the Douglass Street
hillside to the west, 22nd Street to the south, and Church Street to the
east. Its housing stock is heavily Victorian and Edwardian, and in city
data the neighborhood appears as "Castro/Upper Market."
