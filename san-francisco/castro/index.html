Castro / Eureka Valley

One neighborhood in central San Francisco, known by more than one name. It sits roughly between Market Street to the northeast, the Douglass Street hillside to the west, 22nd Street to the south, and Church Street to the east, and its housing stock is heavily Victorian and Edwardian.

Castro or Eureka Valley?

Both — they are the same place. Eureka Valley is the older name, for the valley the neighborhood occupies; the Castro is what almost everyone calls it now, after its main street. This site files addresses under "Castro" and notes Eureka Valley alongside it.
